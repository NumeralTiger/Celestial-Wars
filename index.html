How to Play

Movement: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to navigate.

Shoot & Charge: Use spacebar to fire. Hold it when using Charged Beam (Weapon 5).

Weapons (1-5):

1: Laser

2: Spread Shot

3: Seeker Missile

4: Shockwave

5: Charged Beam

Destroy asteroids for score; don't collide or you lose lives.
